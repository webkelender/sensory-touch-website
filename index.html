About Us

Helping Little Hands Grow, Little Minds Learn

Welcome to Dr. Devyani's Sensory Touch – Occupational Therapy & Learning Centre, where every child is supported to learn, grow, and achieve their fullest potential.

Founded by Dr. Devyani Ajay Gaikwad, a dedicated Pediatric Occupational Therapist with 4 years of hands-on clinical experience, our centre provides evidence-based, child-centered occupational therapy in a caring and engaging environment.

We specialize in supporting children with developmental delays, Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD), ADHD, sensory processing difficulties, learning challenges, and motor skill delays. Through individualized therapy plans, play-based interventions, and close collaboration with families, we help children build independence, confidence, and essential life skills.

At Dr. Devyani's Sensory Touch, we believe that every child is unique, and with the right guidance and support, every child can thrive.

What We Offer

Therapy built around your child

Every programme is tailored to the child in front of us — not a fixed curriculum.

🤲

Occupational Therapy

One on one therapy session supporting children to develop the skills needed for everyday activities, improving independence, motor coordination, sensory processing, and self-care.

Sensory Integration Therapy

Helps children process and respond to sensory information more effectively, improving attention, self-regulation, coordination, and participation in daily activities through structured sensory experiences.

Play & Engagement Therapy

Uses play-based activities to encourage communication, social interaction, emotional expression, and active participation in learning.

🧠

Neurodevelopmental Therapy

Focuses on improving posture, movement, balance, and motor control to support functional development and daily activities.

Oral Placement Therapy

Strengthens oral muscles and improves jaw, lip, and tongue coordination to support clearer speech and safe feeding skills.

💬

Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT)

Helps children recognize and manage thoughts, emotions, and behaviors, building coping strategies, confidence, and emotional resilience.

👥

Group Therapy

Provides a supportive environment where children learn teamwork, communication, cooperation, and social interaction through guided group activities.

🤝

Social Skills Training

Teaches essential social behaviors such as conversation, turn-taking, emotional understanding, problem-solving, and building meaningful relationships.

🌟

Activities of Daily Living (ADL) Training

Develops practical self-care skills like dressing, eating, grooming, toileting, and personal hygiene to promote greater independence.

Good to Know

Frequently asked questions

We see children from toddlers through the early school years — typically anywhere development, sensory processing, or motor skills need extra support. If you're unsure whether your child's age fits, just give us a call and we're happy to talk it through.

Your first visit is an assessment, not a full therapy session. Dr. Devyani spends time observing your child, listening to your concerns, and going through their developmental history — then talks you through what a personalised therapy plan could look like.

Payment is handled directly at the clinic. If you need documentation for insurance reimbursement, just ask — call ahead and we'll let you know what we can provide.

Each standard session lasts exactly 45 minutes. We carefully structure this time to focus on your child's specific goals and ensure you get maximum value. To make the most of your session, we recommend arriving 5 minutes before your scheduled start time.

We're open Monday to Saturday, 10:00 AM to 9:30 PM. We're closed on Sundays.
